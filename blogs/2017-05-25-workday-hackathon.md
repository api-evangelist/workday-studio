<p>What’s different about a Workday Hackathon? For one, a Jedi battles Darth Vader.  <br />
Check out this video:</p>



<p>The Workday Hackathon is an annual 24 hour event where workmates get to work on whatever project they would like. The only rule: <em>Don’t do your day job</em>.</p>

<p>With only the mere essentials provided (food, caffeine, and commemorative t-shirts), the hacker codes at speed and does whatever it takes to get the job done. Despite the exhaustion of an all-nighter, the hacker creates remarkable feats of engineering and shares the results on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WHRWr7QqQCU&amp;feature=youtu.be">YouTube</a>.</p>

<p>Our hackers have now returned to their day jobs, building exciting new features for our Workday customers.</p>
