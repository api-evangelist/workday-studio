<p>Would you like to <strong>VISIT</strong> the Workday office in Dublin?</p>

<p>Would you like to <strong>KNOW</strong> how Workday delivers Software?</p>

<p>Would you like to <strong>MEET</strong> some of the teams involved in scaling the Workday platform?</p>

<p>Would you like to <strong>TALK</strong> to some of the engineers working in the Dublin office?</p>

<p>Would you like to <strong>LEARN</strong> about the latest technologies used in Workday?</p>

<p>If you are a software engineer, DevOps or software developer and your answer is YES to the previous questions you’ll be happy to know that Workday is hosting our first <a href="https://www.eventbrite.ie/e/platform-engineering-open-house-tickets-38834149988">Open House in our Dublin office on the evening of 9th November</a>. This will be a small, intimate event where you can meet and talk in-depth one-to-one. David Clarke, Senior VP of Technology Development, will talk about the technology we are building, now chosen by over 150 of the world’s Fortune 500 companies. Our senior developers will talk in one-to-one sessions through the details of their world at Workday, including the challenges faced and software technologies used.</p>

<p>Join us, meet the following teams in the Platform Engineering group and chat about what they build and the technologies used:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Monitoring, Insights and Alerting: Using Prometheus, Sensu, Kubernetes, Docker.</li>
  <li>Logging and Stats Development: Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana, Hadoop, Kafka.</li>
  <li>Cloud Engineering: OpenStack, OpenContrail.</li>
  <li>Production Engineering: Ruby and REST.</li>
  <li>Operations Engineering: Chef.</li>
  <li>Pipeline Engineering: AWS, Scala, Build &amp; Test Automation.</li>
</ul>
