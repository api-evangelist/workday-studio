<p>Join us at Workday Dublin for the October Functional Kats Meetup. There will be a talk from Juan Manuel Serrano on Type classes in Scala. Juan is a busy man, CTO of Habla Computing, founder and manager of Madrid Scala Meetup group and an organiser of <a href="http://www.lambda.world/">lambda.world</a>.
There will also be a lightning talk from Jeremie Jost and a talk on Javascript and Elm.</p>
