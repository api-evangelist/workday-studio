Workday presents three distinct paths for developers to build enterprise AI agents with built-in safety mechanisms: hosting their own agents with Agent-Ready Tools, running agents on Workday's platform with a preferred front-end, or using Sana, Workday's full AI workspace. The article emphasizes that effective agentic development requires guardrails embedded at the platform level rather than relying on language models to follow policy guidelines. Each path maintains consistent security, compliance, and audit capabilities across Workday's HR and finance systems.
