Workday's DevCon conference unveiled new developer platform capabilities focused on AI agents, with nearly 10,000 developers attending virtually and in-person. CTO Gabe Monroy emphasized maintaining security while accelerating development speed, introducing agent-ready tools and partnerships designed to embed agentic AI capabilities directly into Workday's system. The announcements focused on making Workday an open, agentic platform for enterprise developers.
